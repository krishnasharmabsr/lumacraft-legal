ZenFlow Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026
Developer: LumaCraft Studio
Contact: lumacraftstudio.support@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how ZenFlow ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the ZenFlow mobile application.

1. Information we process

ZenFlow is designed to work primarily offline. The app stores timer and ambient-audio preferences locally on your device so your sessions can remember your settings.

  • focus, short break, and long break durations
  • cycle preferences and auto-start options
  • ambient sound selection and sound-enabled preference

We do not create user accounts, and we do not operate a backend service to collect or store your personal data.

2. Local storage

ZenFlow saves your selected settings locally on your device using local app storage. These settings are used only to power the timer experience inside the app.

3. Third-party services

ZenFlow does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or account systems in the current version. The app currently uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Fonts, which may request font files from Google servers when rendering app typography. These requests may expose standard network information such as IP address and request headers to Google. You can review Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Because ZenFlow does not collect personal data through its own service, we do not share personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes.

5. Children's privacy

ZenFlow is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Security

ZenFlow is designed to minimize privacy risk by keeping your preferences local to your device. The security of locally stored information depends in part on the security of your device and operating system.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, contact lumacraftstudio.support@gmail.com.
